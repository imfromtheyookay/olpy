Must be fixed to FRONT and REAR (or REAR of trailer) and plate must be fixed VERTICALLY (or as close as possible). It is NOT PERMITTED for a plate to be displayed in front or rear screen.

Call grades identify the urgency of a call and the expected response time.
I
Immediate
The immediate presence of a police officer will have a significant impact on the outcome. Typically where there is, or is likely to be, a danger to life, serious threat of violence, serious damage to property or serious injury.
⏱ 15 MINUTES
🚨 SIRENS & ELS
S
Significant
There is a degree of importance or urgency associated with the initial police action, but an emergency response is not required.
⏱ 60 MINUTES
🔵 ELS ONLY
E
Extended
Police attendance is required, but an emergency response is not. No lights or sirens needed.
⏱ 48 HOURS
🟢 NO ELS OR SIRENS
R
Referred
Police attendance is not required. The caller has been dealt with appropriately by the call handler.
⛔ NO RESPONSE NEEDED
